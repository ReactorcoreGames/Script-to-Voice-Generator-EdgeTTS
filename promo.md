## Title

Script to Voice Generator

## Subtitle

Turn author scripts into fully voiced audio files with Edge-TTS, per-character effects, and smart merging.

## Description

Script to Voice Generator converts formatted `.txt` or `.md` script files written by you into fully voiced audio files using Microsoft Edge-TTS — 300+ voices, unlimited, no extra costs, no API keys needed.

Each speaker in your script gets their own voice, pitch, speed, and audio effects. Assign Radio, Reverb, Distortion, Telephone, Robot Voice, Cheap Mic, Underwater, Megaphone, Worn Tape, Intercom, Alien Voice, or Cave effects per character at Off / Mild / Medium / Strong levels. Two bonus toggles — FMSU (brutal digital corruption) and Reverse — round out the toolkit. Combine effects freely for distinct character identities.

While Edge-TTS provides *only* 322 voices, the different settings can make them sounds like 10000+ different people. While Edge-TTS is pre-AI tech, its neural-based results are still pretty dang good, though not on the same level as something like Elevenlabs' V3 TTS or Microsoft's Dragon HD or Google's Chirp 3 HD.

Still, pretty enjoyable to listen to nonetheless and fairly human like, if slightly un-emotional/monotone by comparison.

**The generator produces:**
- Individual audios per each spoken line in the script, original clean version without effects.
- Individual audios per each spoken line in the script, altered with chosen audio filter effects.
- All (effects-enabled) individual audios merged into a fully edited and smartly paced audio file, not normalized. (true audio, better for media/games)
- All (effects-enabled) individual audios merged into a fully edited and smartly paced audio file, yes normalized. (even audio, better for podcasts)
- Reference .txt file with filenames, line numbers and contents that were voiced on those lines.
(All produced audio files are .mp3, 48000 kHz, 64 kbps)

**Features:**
- 300+ Edge-TTS neural voices (English and multilingual)
- Per-character voice, pitch, speed, volume, and audio effects
- 12 audio effects with 4 levels each — combinable
- 2 bonus toggles: FMSU (brutal corruption) and Reverse
- Yell Impact mode for punchy single-word exclamations
- Inner thoughts filter (Whisper, Dreamlike, Dissociated presets)
- Sound effect events (play/stop/loop) placed in the merge timeline
- Smart merged audio with configurable punctuation-based pause timing
- Loudness-normalized and raw merge outputs
- Per-line clip files (clean and effects) for editing flexibility
- Character profiles saved automatically between sessions
- Parse log with line-by-line error reporting
- Included example scripts and AI prompt templates to get started fast

**Ideal for:** 
- Game dialogue
- Character oneliners
- Audio dramas
- Audio books
- Ambient narration
- Meditations audios
- Voice banks

...and any project where you want to convert a written script into audio.
Its unlimited, good quality and all fully automated.
