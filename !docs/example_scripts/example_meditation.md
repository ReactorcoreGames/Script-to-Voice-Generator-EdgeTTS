# You Have Arrived

/*
  A guided meditation session. The speaker addresses the listener directly,
  warmly and without rush. Single speaker, no story — pure comfort and presence.
  Demonstrates: second-person address, slow pacing through pauses and punctuation,
  inner thoughts as the listener's implied inner voice, soft SFX as atmosphere.

  Suggested voice settings:
    Guide - warm female or soft male voice, Reverb: Mild, Cheap Mic: Off
    Pitch: Normal or slightly High
    Speed: -10% (noticeably slower, unhurried)
    Volume: 90% (slightly pulled back, intimate)

  Inner thoughts preset: Whisper (Tab 4)
    These represent the listener's own quiet inner voice emerging during the session.

  Sound channels:
    c1 = soft ambient tone / singing bowl (loop)
    c2 = single bowl strike (once, for transitions)
*/

{play singing_bowl_ambient.mp3, c1, loop}

(3.0)

Guide: You made it here.

(2.0)

Guide: That's enough. Whatever the day has asked of you, whatever is still waiting for you on the other side of this -- right now, none of that is your job.

(2.5)

Guide: Your only job, for the next few minutes, is to be here.

(3.0)

Guide: Let's start with something simple. Take a breath in... and let it go slowly.

(3.5)

Guide: Good. Again.

(3.0)

Guide: Notice how that feels. Not how it should feel. How it actually feels, right now, in your body.

(2.5)

Guide: ((Maybe a little tight. Maybe tired.))

(1.5)

Guide: That's okay. You don't have to fix anything. You just have to notice.

(3.0)

{play bowl_strike.wav, c2, once}

(2.0)

Guide: I want you to think of somewhere that feels safe to you. It doesn't have to be real. It doesn't have to be beautiful. It just has to be yours.

(2.5)

Guide: Maybe it's a room. Maybe it's outside somewhere. Maybe it's a moment more than a place -- a particular kind of light, or quiet, or warmth.

(3.0)

Guide: Take your time finding it.

(4.0)

Guide: ((There it is.))

(2.0)

Guide: Let yourself be there for a moment. You're allowed.

(4.0)

Guide: I want you to know something, and I want you to really let it in.

(2.0)

Guide: You have been carrying a great deal. More than you talk about. More than most people see.

(2.5)

Guide: And you've kept going anyway. Not because it's been easy. Because you're the kind of person who keeps going.

(3.0)

Guide: ((I don't always give myself credit for that.))

(1.5)

Guide: You should. You really should.

(3.0)

{play bowl_strike.wav, c2, once}

(2.0)

Guide: Let your shoulders drop a little. Just... let them fall where they want to be.

(2.5)

Guide: And your jaw. Unclench it, if you've been clenching it. You probably have.

(2.0)

Guide: There. That's yours again.

(3.5)

Guide: I'm going to ask you one question, and I don't want you to answer it out loud. I just want you to sit with it.

(2.0)

Guide: What would it feel like to be a little kinder to yourself today?

(5.0)

Guide: ((I don't always know how to do that.))

(1.5)

Guide: Most of us don't. But you can start small. A little more patience. A little less of the voice that says you should be doing better.

(2.5)

Guide: You're doing something right now. You're here. You're breathing. You're listening.

(2.0)

Guide: That counts.

(4.0)

{play bowl_strike.wav, c2, once}

(2.0)

Guide: When you're ready, start to come back. Gently. No rush.

(2.5)

Guide: Take one more breath in...

(2.0)

Guide: And let it go.

(3.0)

Guide: The day is still there. The list is still there. But so are you -- a little softer now, maybe. A little more yours.

(2.5)

Guide: Go easy out there.

(4.0)

{stop c1}
